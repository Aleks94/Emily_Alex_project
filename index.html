About Us

I gained more interest in what I'm eating when starting University and having to PROPERLY cook for myself. Quickly I understood that homemade food doesnt have to be boring, and neither should you have to look forward to eating out everytime you want something good! Whilst we want to encourage people to cook more, we also dont want anyone to miss out on the incredibles food inspirations and trends around London. Thus, we want to spread the action of gathering great dishes from across the capital whilst encouraging how you can try them at home. Take it and make it!

Emily

"No one is born a great cook, one learns by doing".

Julia Child, Chef & Author

My journey with food started at my family house, full of flavours and ingredients sourced locally from my grandmother’s garden and local farms. I value quality food, and even now, splitting the time between my own family, a cat and busy start-up job, I always find the time to prepare a fresh meal. My current favourites are butternut squash lasagne, sweet potato curry and black bean brownies. Yes, I’m vegetarian. Could you tell? Occasionally, I like challenge myself to something more complicated, and more time consuming. I love replicating the look of the dishes form the cook books with my own twist

Alex
